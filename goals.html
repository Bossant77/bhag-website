My Goals

The list I'm chasing

1. Build something that 1M people use

Not for the vanity number. For the discipline of building something so simple, so useful, that it spreads on its own.

2. Speak three languages fluently

Spanish (done), English (done), and a third — likely Mandarin or Portuguese. Languages multiply who you can talk to and what you can read.

3. Run a sub-3-hour marathon

Not because I love running, but because I want to know I can train my body the way I train my mind.

4. Mentor 100 people who go on to do something hard

Impact compounds. One good push at the right moment can change a trajectory. I want to push 100 trajectories.

5. Live in 5 countries before 30

Different countries, different ways of seeing the world. Comfort is the enemy of perspective.
