Big Hairy Audacious Goals

Why aim small when you can aim huge?

A BHAG (Big Hairy Audacious Goal) is a goal so bold it forces you to rethink who you are. It is uncomfortable, ambitious, and the kind of thing you only achieve if you commit fully.

I am Santiago. I believe in doing hard things on purpose. The point of a BHAG is not just the destination — it is the version of yourself you have to become to get there.
